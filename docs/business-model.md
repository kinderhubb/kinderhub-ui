# KinderHub Business Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Transaction Fees

Primary revenue source.

- Commission on each booking (10-15%)
- Charged to providers
- Transparent fee structure

### 2. Provider Subscriptions (Future)

Tiered subscription plans for providers:

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Basic | Free | List up to 5 activities, standard visibility |
| Pro | €29/month | Unlimited activities, featured placement, analytics |
| Business | €79/month | Multi-location, team access, priority support, marketing tools |

### 3. Featured Listings (Future)

- Promoted activities in search results
- Homepage featured spots
- Category sponsorships

### 4. Premium Parent Features (Future)

- Early access to popular activities
- Exclusive provider discounts
- Advanced scheduling tools
