# KinderHub User Personas

## Parent Personas

### 1. Sarah — The Busy Professional

**Demographics:**
- Age: 35
- Occupation: Marketing Manager
- Children: 2 (ages 5 and 8)
- Location: Urban

**Goals:**
- Find activities that fit her busy schedule
- Book quickly without phone calls or emails
- Manage both children's activities in one place

**Pain Points:**
- Limited time for research
- Juggling multiple provider calendars
- Last-minute schedule changes

**Behaviors:**
- Books primarily via mobile
- Values reviews and recommendations
- Willing to pay premium for convenience

---

### 2. Michael — The Engaged Dad

**Demographics:**
- Age: 42
- Occupation: Software Engineer (Remote)
- Children: 1 (age 6)
- Location: Suburban

**Goals:**
- Find unique, educational activities
- Develop his child's interests
- Balance screen time with real-world activities

**Pain Points:**
- Overwhelmed by options
- Unsure which activities suit his child
- Wants quality assurance

**Behaviors:**
- Researches thoroughly before booking
- Reads detailed descriptions and reviews
- Prefers desktop for initial research

---

### 3. Emma — The Budget-Conscious Mom

**Demographics:**
- Age: 29
- Occupation: Teacher
- Children: 3 (ages 3, 6, 9)
- Location: Semi-rural

**Goals:**
- Find affordable activities
- Discover free community events
- Manage costs across multiple children

**Pain Points:**
- Limited budget for activities
- Fewer options in her area
- Transportation challenges

**Behaviors:**
- Compares prices carefully
- Books during promotions
- Values multi-child discounts
